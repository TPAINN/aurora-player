How it works

Three steps to the full experience.

01

Search any song

Type artist and track. Aurora searches iTunes, Genius, and YouTube simultaneously to find the best match in under a second.

02

Aurora analyzes

Album art colors are extracted live. Synced lyrics load from LrcLib or Genius. BPM is computed in real-time from the audio stream.

03

Feel the song

Every word lights up on cue. The background breathes with the beat. Chorus entries burst with expanding energy rings.
